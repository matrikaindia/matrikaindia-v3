  • Child Birth Connection
    Childbirth Connection is a United States not-for-profit organization founded in 1918 as Maternity Center Association. Their mission is to improve the quality of maternity care through research, education, advocacy and policy. Childbirth Connection promotes safe, effective and satisfying evidence-based maternity care and provides valuable information for childbearing women and their families.

  • Birth On A Labor Day
    It is the website for BOLDBIRTH (Birth on Labor Day). Birth is a play based on over one hundred interviews New York playwright Karen Brody conducted with mothers across America who gave birth between 2000-2004. It tells the true stories of 8 women painting a portrait of how low-risk, educated women are giving birth in America today. Birth was performed in cities throughout the US on their Labor Day September 1. In 2006 Matrika organized a performance in Delhi. It is amazing how middle and upperclass Indian women's experiences in childbirth are similar to those in the US.

  • India: A second opinion; Frontline
    This series of articles and videos gives an introduction into Ayurveda, from an outsider's point of view. Knowledge of Ayurveda is helpful in understanding many indigenous midwifery practices, even though Vaidyas do not conduct births. The center described in Coimbatore is beginning to offer Ayurvedic obstetrical services. They have found that their providing pre-natal care is not sufficient to guarantee a 'good birth' within other hospitals with obstetricians who practice bio-medicine.
